Attributes range from -3 to +3. When you roll to resolve a move, add the number associated with the required attribute.

## List of Attributes
- [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Vigor\|Vigor]] represents your physical power and constitution. It is used in tasks that involve having to fight up close, push, pull, or endure.
- [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Silver\|Silver]] represents your motor control and reflexes. It is used in tasks that involve having to run, dodge, sneak, or maneuver.
- [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ether\|Ether]] represents your mental acumen and knowledge. It is used in tasks that involve having to deduce, learn, plan, or analyze.
- [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Humor\|Humor]] represents your allure and wisdom. It is used in tasks that involve having to intuit, manipulate, empathize, or command.
- [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ichor\|Ichor]] represents your divine nature. It is used in tasks that involve your godly powers.
