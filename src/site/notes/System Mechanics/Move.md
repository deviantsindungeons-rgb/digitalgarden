A move is a narrative tool for the players to change the current situation. When you want to do something significantly impactful, you'll usually make a move. Consider the details of the fiction and the triggers for moves you have when you decide to make a move. You won't normally have to [[Moves/Basic Moves/Defy Danger\|Defy Danger]] to do some parkour, but if there's a ravenous pack of harpies on your tail, get ready to roll.
