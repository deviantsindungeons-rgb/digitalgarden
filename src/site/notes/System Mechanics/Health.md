Your health represents your physical wellbeing. When you create your character, it is equal to 5 + [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Attributes\|Vigor]]. If you take [[System Mechanics/Combat\|damage]], you lose that much health. Health can be restored through magic, items, and conventional medicine, as well as passively over the course of downtime. When you reach 0 health, [[Moves/Core Moves/Face Death\|Face Death]].
