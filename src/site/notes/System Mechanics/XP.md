When you have 5 XP, increase your level by one and erase all your XP. Choose an advancement from the list below. When you choose an advancement, mark its associated box. If all boxes are marked for that advancement, you cannot choose it.
☐☐ Gain +1 to an [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Attributes\|attribute]] (max. 2).
☐ Unlock a path in an [[System Mechanics/Archetype\|archetype]] you have and gain its starting move.
∞ Gain an advanced move in one of your paths.
∞ Increase your current and maximum [[System Mechanics/Health\|health]] by 1.

From Level 5 onwards, you can also choose from the following:
☐☐ Gain +1 to an attribute (max. 3).
☐ Unlock a path in an archetype you have and gain its starting move.
☐ Unlock an archetype you don't have and gain its starting move.
∞ Unlock a divine move from one of your archetypes. Lock it after using it. 

From Level 10 onwards, you can also choose from the following:
☐☐ Gain +1 to an attribute (max. 3).
☐ Unlock a path in an archetype you have and gain its starting move.
☐ Unlock an archetype you don't have and gain its starting move.
∞ Gain +1 to an attribute (max. 1).
