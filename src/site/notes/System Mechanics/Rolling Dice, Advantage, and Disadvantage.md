Whenever you are indicated to roll, roll 2d6 and add whichever modifier is required. 
- When you have advantage, roll 3d6 and keep the highest two results. 
- When you have disadvantage, roll 3d6 and keep the lowest two results. 

Sources of advantage and disadvantage can stack and cancel each other out, but the amount of dice you roll will not change.
