Conditions reflect the inner turmoil your character goes through. They impose penalties on the results of your moves, so try to avoid incurring too many of them.  You accrue conditions by making [[System Mechanics/Move\|moves]]. If a move asks you to mark a condition that is already marked, you cannot make that move. Consider your character's temperament and behaviour through the lens of your currently marked conditions. 

Conditions can immediately be cleared through the effects of certain moves and through someone else's [[Moves/Basic Moves/Support\|support]]. Conditions can also be cleared through taking decisive action, but doing so only clears the condition after the end of the current [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]].

When you mark your last condition, [[Moves/Core Moves/Reach Your Limit\|Reach Your Limit]]. Conditions can become locked through the consequences of reaching your limit. When a condition is locked, it cannot be cleared through getting support or taking decisive action, instead, specific moves are required to unlock it, or enough downtime. If all of your conditions are locked and you reach your limit and you *don't* roll a 10+, start drafting up a new character...

### Frightened
While you have this condition, take -3 to moves rolled with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Attributes\|Vigor]]. To clear it decisively, run away when your allies need you. You may lose [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with other PCs as per the GM's discretion.

### Guilty
While you have this condition, take -3 to moves rolled with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Attributes\|Silver]]. To clear it decisively, endanger yourself or your allies. You may lose [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with other PCs as per the GM's discretion.

### Angry
While you have this condition, take -3 to moves rolled with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Attributes\|Ether]]. To clear it decisively, lash out at your allies or break something important. You may lose [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with other PCs as per the GM's discretion.

### Troubled
While you have this condition, take -3 to moves rolled with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Attributes\|Humor]]. To clear it decisively, take foolhardy action without consulting anyone. You may lose [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with other PCs as per the GM's discretion.

### Drained
While you have this condition, take -3 to moves rolled with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Attributes\|Ichor]]. To clear it decisively, give up when it matters most. You may lose [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with other PCs as per the GM's discretion.
