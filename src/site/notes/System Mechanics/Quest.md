A quest is a task assigned to you by someone else. It might seem trivial or unnecessary to you, but think of it as an opportunity to prove yourself. Should you succeed, you can gain powerful rewards - magic items, rare weaponry, hints of hard times in your future, divine favour, etc. - depending on who assigned the quest and who's watching. If a random demigod asks you to watch their back while they prank someone else, it's a quest. If the oracle tells you to travel across the country and kill a legendary monster, it's a quest.
