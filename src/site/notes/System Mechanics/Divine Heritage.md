You are the product of a god, an immensely powerful figure tied to mythology and the fabric of reality itself. When you create your character, choose a figure from the Greco-Roman pantheon - that's your progenitor - whether they're your direct parent, creator, benefactor, etc. Figure out the details of how and incorporate them into your backstory.

Your progenitor has a domain of influence that you draw your powers from. Children of more powerful primordial figures are more likely to have powers of a higher magnitude, but they are also more likely to attract the attention of dangerous monsters. Powers can also be mixed and altered through multiple lineages. Perhaps your bloodline descends from an ancient cult of Athena, but your immediate parent is Ares himself. Think about how your powers would manifest and how they could be used effectively.

You won't get any specific powers when you create your character, but when you make certain [[System Mechanics/Move\|moves]] like [[Moves/Basic Moves/Defy Danger\|Defy Danger]], [[Moves/Basic Moves/Pray\|Pray]], or [[Moves/Basic Moves/Unleash Your Powers\|Unleash Your Powers]], describe your vision of how things play out and incorporate your powers into it. Over time, you'll develop unique moves and abilities that can be utilized more readily.
