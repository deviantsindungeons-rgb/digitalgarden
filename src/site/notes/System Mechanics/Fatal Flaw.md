No hero is perfect. In fact, most heroes are far from and are riddled with internal struggles. Your fatal flaw represents something that is constantly hanging over your head - in a stressful situation, it can spell your doom. However, confronting your fatal flaw in a time of peril can elevate you to new heights. When you create your character, describe your fatal flaw and write down some details about it :
- Is there a significant memory or experience tied to it? 
- What kind of choices and circumstances does it become most apparent in?
- (Optional) Does another PC know about it? How has it affected your [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|relationship]] with them?
When you fail at something or things don't go your way because of your Fatal Flaw, gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].
