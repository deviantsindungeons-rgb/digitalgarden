# Starting Move: Open Your Heart
You always find a way to keep going. You have a pool of Heart equal to your [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Humor\|Humor]] (min. 0). When you lose [[System Mechanics/Health\|health]], you may lose that much Heart instead. When you open your heart, increase your Heart by one, to a max of your maximum health.
- When you gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|advancement]] and increase your health, open your heart.
- When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Forge a Bond\|Forge a Bond]], open your heart.
- When you decrease an attribute (through [[Moves/Core Moves/Face Death\|facing death]], [[Moves/Core Moves/Reach Your Limit\|reaching your limit]], or something else), open your heart.
- (If you have the Empath path) The first time in a [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]] that another PC gains [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with you, open your heart.
- (If you have the Eudaemonic path) The first time in a scene that you unlock a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]], open your heart.
- (If you have the Excubitor path) The first time in a scene that you become [[System Mechanics/Combat\|bloodied]], open your heart.

# Divine Moves
*Start with all divine moves locked - you don't have access to them. After level 5, when you gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|advancement]], you can unlock a divine move. When you use it, lock it again.*
- Divine Determination. When you take damage that would kill you or force you to [[Moves/Core Moves/Face Death\|face death]], you may refuse it. Describe how your divine blood surges to keep you standing. Unlock and clear a condition and restore your [[System Mechanics/Health\|health]] until you are no longer [[System Mechanics/Combat\|bloodied]].
- Bleeding Heart. Describe how your devotion swells and encompasses your allies. You and all your [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|bond-mates]] in the same scene gain three Heart.
- Take it All. Describe how you draw the entire focus of a situation onto yourself. For the next few moments, monsters will only go after you, NPCs are enraptured by you, or something else.
