*You might be an emotional trash can for your friends, but you take pride in it.*

### Starting Move: Punching Bag
In battle, use an action to mark a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] in place of a PC that is very close to you or to [[Moves/Basic Moves/Resist a Powerful Effect\|Resist a Powerful Effect]] in their place. 

### Advanced Move: Show Solidarity
When a [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|bond-mate]] locks a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]], you may mark it (or lock it if it's marked). They do not suffer penalties from that condition for the rest of the [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]].

### Advanced Move: Grit your Teeth
If you have more [[System Mechanics/Condition\|conditions]] marked than Heart, suffer -2 to rolls from conditions instead of -3.

### Advanced Move: You're Not Alone
When you listen to someone vent about something you've been through yourself, roll + [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Humor\|Humor]]. On a 7+, share your experience and clear a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]]. On a 10+, they warm up to you and you gain +1 when you next try to [[Moves/Basic Moves/Exert Influence\|Exert Influence]] on them. On a 6-, misjudge what to share and drive a wedge between the two of you. Gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].
