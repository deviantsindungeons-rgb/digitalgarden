*Use your passion to help your allies and conquer your enemies.*

### Starting Move: Share the Love
You can use Heart when very close PCs of your choice take [[System Mechanics/Combat\|damage]].

### Advanced Move: Ceaseless Edge
While bloodied, deal 1 extra damage when you [[System Mechanics/Combat\|exchange damage]].

### Advanced Move: Rage-bait
When you roll a 12+ to [[Moves/Combat Moves/Distract an Enemy\|Distract an Enemy]] by provoking them, make an extra choice. You can make the same choice multiple times.

### Advanced Move: Channel Rage
When you [[Moves/Combat Moves/All Out Attack\|All Out Attack]] an enemy that made you or a [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|bond-mate]] [[Moves/Basic Moves/Resist a Powerful Effect\|Resist a Powerful Effect]], roll with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Humor\|Humor]] as well and take the roll of your choice.
