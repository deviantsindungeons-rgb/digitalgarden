*You brighten up a room just by walking in.*

### Starting Move: Pull Through
When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Provide Aid\|Provide Aid]], mark an additional condition to give an extra boost. You can choose an option that has been chosen by another PC. When you add your [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]], the minimum amount you add is equal to your [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Humor\|Humor]] (min. 1).

### Advanced Move: Bless
When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Provide Aid\|Provide Aid]], you can also choose to add your Heart (max. 3).

### Advanced Move: Stop the Show
When you [[Moves/Combat Moves/Enter Battle\|Enter Battle]] by doing something extra cringy or otherwise wholly unnecessary, roll with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Humor\|Humor]].  On a 7+, apologize to absolutely nobody. Ignore penalties from being [[System Mechanics/Condition\|troubled]] for the rest of the fight. On a 6-, watch in horror as your plans work against you and gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].

### Advanced Move: Live Vicariously
When a [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|bond-mate]] [[Moves/Core Moves/Face Death\|Faces Death]], add your current [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with them to your Heart.
