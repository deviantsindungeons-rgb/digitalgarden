*You're prepared for every possibility, every outcome, every little detail-*

### Starting Move: Masterminding
Gain a pool of Prep equal to your [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ether\|Ether]]. When you start a session, reset the pool to your Ether. Spend Prep 1:1 to:
- shift your alignment in a direction of your choice.
- gain +1 to the roll when you roll with a principle.
- take an additional action [[System Mechanics/Combat\|in a fight]].

### Advanced Move: Flashback
When you retroactively declare how you prepared for something in advance, mark [[System Mechanics/Condition\|conditions]] or spend Prep as determined by the GM using the guideline below. As a consequence during a flashback the GM may inflict extra conditions.
- 1: Ordinary action, easy opportunity, no rolls required, a short time ago.
- 2: Complex action, unlikely opportunity, one roll required, some time ago.
- 3: Elaborate action, special opportunity, multiple rolls required, a long time ago.

### Advanced Move: Read Between the Lines
Once per [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]] when anybody [[Moves/Basic Moves/Assess\|Assesses]] something, spend a Prep to answer a question instead of the GM.

### Advanced Move: Break It Down
When you encounter an unfamiliar mechanism or phenomena and try to understand its underlying processes, roll with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ether\|Ether]]. On a 7+, figure out a way it could be useful and gain a Prep. On a 6-, the answers elude you - you'll have to try again later.
