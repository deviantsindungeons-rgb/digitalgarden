*Achieving inner peace is by no means easy, but it's a mountain that you are prepared to summit.*

### Starting Move: Recalibrate
When you take decisive action in line with your highest principle, return to your centre and immediately unlock and clear the associated condition.

### Advanced Move: Watch and Learn
When you seek guidance from a trusted NPC or successfully receive [[Moves/Basic Moves/Support\|Support]], choose a principle. When you next roll a 7+ with that principle, gain a [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with a PC of your choice as you share your teachings.

### Advanced Move: Take a Moment
Once per [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]], when you meditate, roll with a principle of your choice. On a 7+, unlock and clear a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]]. On a 6-, shift your alignment towards centre and gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].

### Advanced Move: Union of Minds
When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Support\|Support]] someone, additionally ask if they identify with your highest principle. If you roll with that principle, you don't suffer any penalties on a 6-.
