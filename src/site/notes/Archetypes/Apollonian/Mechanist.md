*Who said you needed a plan to make shit happen?*

### Starting Move: Let Me See That
When you quickly tinker with equipment, mark a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] and choose one:
- Mix in a little something and restore a portion to a unit of [[Items/Magic Items\|ambrosia or nectar]].
- Polish and repair a piece of [[Items/Armour\|defensive equipment]], restoring a use.
- Tune up a [[Items/Weapons\|weapon]]. It deals 1 extra damage for the rest of the [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]].

### Advanced Move: Make It Work
When you have time to examine your surroundings and build a useful contraption, describe what you're looking for and roll + [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ether\|Ether]]. 
- On a 10+, you cobble together exactly what you need - name it and describe it.
- On a 7-9, you make something useful but limited - choose one:
	- It takes longer than expected.
	- It only works once.
	- It requires a use from equipment you have on hand.
	- It has a critical flaw that you discover at the worst moment.
- On a 6-, you make something that works, but the GM chooses two from the above list. Gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].

### Advanced Move: Tandem Tactics
When a PC fails to [[Moves/Combat Moves/All Out Attack\|All Out Attack]] or [[Moves/Combat Moves/Take Control\|Take Control]] of something after someone else has created a temporary opportunity for them, find a way to keep hope alive. Another PC can take advantage of the same opportunity to make the same move, but it must done before the start of your next turn.

### Advanced Move: Manifest a Miracle
When you say a terrible situation is still somehow going “according to plan”, create a significant positive twist (an enemy on your side, hidden reinforcements, smuggled equipment, a trap, etc.). You’re still in some trouble, but not nearly as much as it seemed. Lose access to this move until you next roll a 10+ when you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Pray\|Pray]].
