*If anyone's going to be left standing after it's all over, it's you.*

### Starting Move: Uncanny Dodge
Once per [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]] while at full [[System Mechanics/Health\|health]], reduce the first instance of [[System Mechanics/Combat\|damage]] you take by your [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Silver\|Silver]] (to a minimum of 1). Refresh this ability when you become bloodied for the first time that scene.

### Advanced Move: Blindside
When you use your expertise to [[Moves/Combat Moves/Distract an Enemy\|Distract an Enemy]] or [[Moves/Combat Moves/Take Control\|Take Control]] of something material, on a 10+, make an extra choice *and* avoid their retaliation.

### Advanced Move: Leave No Trace
When you and your allies are on the run and leave an area behind, you may attempt to obfuscate the trail and roll + [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ether\|Ether]]. On a 7+, leave no trace of your presence. On a 10+, plant a fake trail that will buy you more time. On a 6-, leave a clue that can be used to track you without realizing.

### Advanced Move: Go Poof
When you would [[Moves/Basic Moves/Resist a Powerful Effect\|Resist a Powerful Effect]], mark [[System Mechanics/Condition\|guilty]] to disappear and reappear far away, avoiding it entirely. If it's already marked, lock it. You cannot use this move again until you unlock and clear it.
