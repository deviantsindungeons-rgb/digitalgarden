*Where would your friends be without you?*

### Starting Move: Always Prepared
When you learn of an obstacle or threat ahead, mark [[System Mechanics/Condition\|troubled]] to change a skill that you have expertise in to one that you didn't have expertise in as you prepare for whats to come. If it's already marked, lock it. You cannot use this move again until you unlock and clear it.

### Advanced Move: Crash Course
When you show someone else the ropes in a skill that you have expertise in, roll + [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Humor\|Humor]]. On a 7+, they gain expertise in that skill for the rest of the [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]] (and the scene after if you split up) and choose one.
- When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Defy Danger\|Defy Danger]] using your expertise and roll a 7+, make one fewer choice.
- When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Assess\|Assess]] by using your expertise and roll a 7+, ask one more question.
On a 6-, describe how your attempt fails. Gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].

### Advanced Move: Heads Up
When a [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|bond-mate]] employs a skill that you have expertise in, your words of advice echo in their mind and they gain +1 to their roll.

### Advanced Move: Lend a Hand
When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Provide Aid\|Provide Aid]] by using your expertise, choose an additional benefit.
