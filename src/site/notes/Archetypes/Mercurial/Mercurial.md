*An elusive and wily scoundrel.*

# Attribute Spread (V/S/E/H/I)
*Choose an array and add 1 to any two different attributes.*
- 0, 1, -1, -1, 0
- -1, 1, 0, 0, -1
- -1, 1, -1, 0, 0

# Starting Move: Gain Expertise
Choose a skill from the following list: acrobatics, deception, investigation, sleight of hand, stealth, or survival. You gain expertise in it - you are considered especially crafty when it comes to tasks that employ that skill. When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Defy Danger\|Defy Danger]] or [[Moves/Basic Moves/Assess\|Assess]] by using your expertise and roll a 7+, make one fewer choice (when you defy danger) and ask one more question (when you assess). When you gain an advanced move from a path in this archetype, choose an additional skill to gain expertise in or work with the GM to develop a custom skill and gain expertise in it.

# Divine Moves
*Start with all divine moves locked - you don't have access to them. After level 5, when you gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|advancement]], you can unlock a divine move. When you use it, lock it again.*
- Divine Determination. When you take damage that would kill you or force you to [[Moves/Core Moves/Face Death\|face death]], you may refuse it. Describe how your divine blood surges to keep you standing. Unlock and clear a condition and restore your [[System Mechanics/Health\|health]] until you are no longer [[System Mechanics/Combat\|bloodied]].
- Flawless Execution. The next time you use your expertise and make a [[System Mechanics/Move\|move]], forgo the roll and treat it as a 12+. Describe how your mastery manifests.
- I Know A Guy. When you visit a new or significantly altered memorable location, describe a contact that you have. The contact will provide an opportunity related to one of your objectives (your choice). Additionally, choose one form of aid they offer: 
	- Information - they answer one question (and any follow-up questions) honestly.
	- Resources - they provide a useful item, money, transportation, etc.
	- A hand - they accompany you for one [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]], acting as a capable ally.
