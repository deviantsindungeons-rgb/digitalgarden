*You've never had a problem you couldn't outrun.*

### Starting Move: Buy Time
When you are forced to act without time to plan, you may [[Moves/Basic Moves/Defy Danger\|Defy Danger]] with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Silver\|Silver]] even if another attribute is demanded. On a 12+, gain [[System Mechanics/Rolling Dice, Advantage, and Disadvantage\|advantage]] on your next move.

### Advanced Move: Flash of Speed
When you roll 10+ with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Silver\|Silver]], choose an ally in the [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]] to impress. They gain one [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with you.

### Advanced Move: Shoot the Gap
When you sneak into or out of a well-guarded area, roll + [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ether\|Ether]]. On a 7+, you additionally find an unexpected shortcut or blind spot that you can use on the way back. On a 6-, it's complicated - the GM explains how.

### Advanced Move: Channel Force
When you [[Moves/Combat Moves/All Out Attack\|All Out Attack]] an enemy that has been [[Moves/Combat Moves/Distract an Enemy\|distracted]], also roll with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Silver\|Silver]] and take the roll of your choice.
