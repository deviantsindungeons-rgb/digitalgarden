*You've been in tough situations all your life. You always find a way to push through.*

- -1, 0, -1, 0, 1

- 1, 0, -1, -1, 0

# Starting Move: Third Eye
When you [[Moves/Combat Moves/Enter Battle\|Enter Battle]] or [[Moves/Basic Moves/Unleash Your Powers\|Unleash Your Powers]], gain an Insight on a 10+. You can also choose "Gain an Insight" when you roll a 7+ on a [[System Mechanics/Move\|combat move]] (other than Enter Battle). Use Insight to gain +1 to a roll with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ichor\|Ichor]] (once per roll). Insight disappears at the end of a [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]].

When you use three Insight in the same scene, you can try to enter the zone. Roll + [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ichor\|Ichor]]:
- On a 10+, choose two:
	- Deal 1 extra [[System Mechanics/Combat\|damage]] when you deal damage for the rest of the scene.
	- Lose 1 less [[System Mechanics/Health\|health]] when you lose health for the rest of the scene.
	- Gain 1 more when you use Insight to gain a bonus to a roll for the rest of the scene.
- On a 7-9, choose one.
- On a 6-, the zone eludes you. Mark a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] and gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].

# Divine Moves
*Start with all divine moves locked - you don't have access to them. After level 5, when you gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|advancement]], you can unlock a divine move. When you use it, lock it again.*
- Divine Determination. When you take damage that would kill you or force you to [[Moves/Core Moves/Face Death\|Face Death]], you may refuse it. Describe how your divine blood surges to keep you standing. Unlock and clear a condition and restore your [[System Mechanics/Health\|health]] until you are no longer [[System Mechanics/Combat\|bloodied]].
- Lock In. When you [[Moves/Combat Moves/Enter Battle\|Enter Battle]], try to enter the zone. Make an additional choice when you roll a 7+ to enter the zone.
- Emperor Eye. While you're in the zone, all your [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|bond-mates]] in the same scene gain the benefits. If they would trigger one of your "Eye" move benefits, gain an Insight.
