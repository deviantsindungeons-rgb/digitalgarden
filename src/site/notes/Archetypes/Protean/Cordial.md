*You're a people person!*

### Starting Move: Teary Eye
When you [[Moves/Combat Moves/Take Control\|Take Control]] or [[Moves/Basic Moves/Support\|Support]], gain an Insight on a 10+. Use Insight to gain +1 to a roll with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Humor\|Humor]] (once per move). Insight disappears at the end of a [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]].

### Advanced Move: Remember your Manners
While you're in the zone and you [[Moves/Combat Moves/Take Control\|Take Control]] of something by outsmarting or tricking your enemy, always avoid their retaliation.

### Advanced Move: Butter Up
When you get someone to lower their guard through kind words, roll + [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Humor\|Humor]]. On a 7+, learn something that can be used [[Moves/Combat Moves/Secure an Edge\|Secure an Edge]] against them in the future. On a 6-, you trigger them somehow and they get aggressive. Gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].

### Advanced Move: Hold it Down
Use an Insight to choose an additional benefit when you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Provide Aid\|Provide Aid]].
