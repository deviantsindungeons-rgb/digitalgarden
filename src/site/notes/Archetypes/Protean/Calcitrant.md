*You're always prepared to stand your ground, no matter what the consequences are.*

### Starting Move: Red Eye
When you [[Moves/Combat Moves/All Out Attack\|All Out Attack]] or [[Moves/Basic Moves/Exert Influence\|Exert Influence]], gain an Insight on a 10+. Use Insight to gain +1 to a roll with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Vigor\|Vigor]] (once per roll). Insight disappears at the end of a [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]].

### Advanced Move: Never Back Down
When you engage in battle with a powerful figure and openly refuse to back down, gain an extra action at the start of each of your first three turns. When you [[Moves/Combat Moves/Distract an Enemy\|Distract this Enemy]], make an extra choice regardless of the result.

### Advanced Move: Sticks and Stones
When you are reprimanded severely for your actions, roll + [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Vigor\|Vigor]]. On a 7+, stick to your guns and gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]]. On a 6-, describe how you regret your actions and mark a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]].

### Advanced Move: Strike Harder
When you [[Moves/Combat Moves/All Out Attack\|All Out Attack]], use an Insight to make an extra choice regardless of the result. You can choose the same option multiple times if you do so.
