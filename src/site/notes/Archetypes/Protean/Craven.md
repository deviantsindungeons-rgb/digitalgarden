*Your fears are especially crippling. Somehow, you always end up facing them.*

### Starting Move: Wide Eye
When you [[Moves/Combat Moves/Distract an Enemy\|Distract an Enemy]] or [[Moves/Basic Moves/Defy Danger\|Defy Danger]], gain an Insight on a 10+. Use Insight to gain +1 to a roll with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Silver\|Silver]] (once per roll). Insight disappears at the end of a [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]].

### Advanced Move: Gaslight
When you [[Moves/Combat Moves/Enter Battle\|Enter Battle]] while [[System Mechanics/Condition\|guilty]], gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]] and an extra action on your first turn. You can additionally clear guilty decisively by landing the final blow on an enemy or taking credit for such a blow.

### Advanced Move: Compartmentalize
When you would mark a specific [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] or lock a condition and you aren't guilty, you can instead mark guilty.

### Advanced Move: Cleanse
Use an Insight to unlock and clear a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] after rolling a 6- on a [[System Mechanics/Move\|move]] affected by it.
