*Almost everything you know came out of a book. Time to put it to use!*

### Starting Move: Learned
You have devoted yourself to understanding the world in all its complexity. Choose two domains of knowledge now. When you would gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|advancement]], you can instead choose two more domains.
- Magic and the Mist
- Magical locations (Mt. Olympus, the Underworld, the Sea of Monsters, etc.)
- Divine figures and their spheres of influence
- Heroes and their legends
- Monsters and their legends
- The Fates and prophecy
- The human side of nature
- The man-made world

When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Assess\|Assess]] something that falls within one of your domains, gain the following benefits:
- On a 10+, ask one additional question from the list or ask a question not on the list - the GM must answer it honestly.
- On a 12+, farm Aura.

### Advanced Move: Don't Go Alone
When you [[Moves/Combat Moves/Enter Battle\|Enter Battle]] against something that falls within one of your domains, you can [[Moves/Basic Moves/Provide Aid\|Provide Aid]] to everyone close to you without marking any conditions. On a 12+, they gain an extra action on their first turn.

### Advanced Move: Educate Yourself
When you encounter something unfamiliar and apply your knowledge, roll + [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ether\|Ether]]. On a 7+, describe what you know about it in relation to your domains - it counts as part of that domain from now on. On a 6-, you come up blank. Mark a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] and gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].

### Advanced Move: Look Back
When you reflect on the absurdity of your life with another PC and [[Moves/Basic Moves/Support\|Support]] them, come up with an additional yes-no question and ask it. On a 10+, they clear an additional [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] *and* gain a [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with you.
