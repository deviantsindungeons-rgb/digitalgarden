*You command power. How will you use it?*

### Starting Move: Make Your Own Luck
When you start a session, roll a d6 as your Lucky Die and write down its result. During play, after you roll, you may switch one die with your Lucky Die. The die that’s switched out becomes your new Lucky Die.

### Advanced Move: Act Selfishly
When you put your own self-interests before your allies', you can [[Moves/Basic Moves/Provide Aid\|Provide Aid]] to yourself without marking a condition. If you choose to add depth, add your [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ichor\|Ichor]] instead. Spend Aura to gain extra benefits 1:1. 

### Advanced Move: Precede Yourself
When you leverage your well-known feats and accomplishments to [[Moves/Basic Moves/Exert Influence\|Exert Influence]], gain [[System Mechanics/Rolling Dice, Advantage, and Disadvantage\|advantage]]. On a 12+, farm Aura.

### Advanced Move: Rally
If a [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|bond-mate]] is [[System Mechanics/Condition\|troubled or drained]], you can spend Aura when they roll. Furthermore, you can switch their die with your Lucky Die.
