# Starting Move: Farm Aura
Only you have an understanding of your true potential, represented by your Aura. You start with a maximum amount of Aura equal to your [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ichor\|Ichor]] (min. 1). When you gain an advanced move in this archetype, increase your maximum amount by one. You can spend Aura to: 
- Gain [[System Mechanics/Rolling Dice, Advantage, and Disadvantage\|advantage]] on a roll.
- When you have [[System Mechanics/Rolling Dice, Advantage, and Disadvantage\|advantage or disadvantage]] on a roll, re-roll a single die.
When you farm Aura, add one to your pool. If your pool is full, clear a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] instead. You farm Aura when:
- You roll a 12+ with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ichor\|Ichor]] or on a [[System Mechanics/Move\|combat move]].
- You [[Moves/Basic Moves/Forge a Bond\|Forge a Bond]].
- You successfully confront your [[System Mechanics/Fatal Flaw\|Fatal Flaw]].
- You complete a [[System Mechanics/Quest\|Quest]].
- You [[Moves/Core Moves/End a Session\|End a Session]] and gain three XP.

# Divine Moves
*Start with all divine moves locked - you don't have access to them. After level 5, when you gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|advancement]], you can unlock a divine move. When you use it, lock it again.*
- Divine Determination. When you take damage that would kill you or force you to [[Moves/Core Moves/Face Death\|face death]], you may refuse it. Describe how your divine blood surges to keep you standing. Unlock and clear a condition and restore your [[System Mechanics/Health\|health]] until you are no longer [[System Mechanics/Combat\|bloodied]].
- Divine Intervention. When you're in a pinch, name a god or powerful divine figure that you've previously encountered and describe what you ask of them. Your Aura has captured their attention and they'll help. They may attach a condition or complication to the favour, but they cannot refuse outright.
- Divine Determinism. Double your current Aura, ignoring the cap for the rest of the [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]]. For the rest of the scene, you can spend Aura on any PC. At the end of the scene, set your Aura to your [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ichor\|Ichor]] (min. 1).
