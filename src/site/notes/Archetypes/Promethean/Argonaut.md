*A life of adventure is all you've dreamed of. Are you prepared?*

### Starting Move: Keep it Down
Choose a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] other than drained or bloodied when you unlock this move. While you have that condition, gain +1 to rolls made when you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Unleash Your Powers\|Unleash Your Powers]]. If that condition is locked, roll with [[System Mechanics/Rolling Dice, Advantage, and Disadvantage\|advantage]].

### Advanced Move: Pick Up the Pieces
When a [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|bond-mate]] rolls a 6- on a move and you follow in their footsteps by making the same move, gain +1 to the roll. On a 10+, farm Aura.

### Advanced Move: Surpass Yourself
When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Defy Danger\|Defy Danger]] or [[Moves/Basic Moves/Resist a Powerful Effect\|Resist a Powerful Effect]] by attempting a feat beyond the scope of your mortal body, like lifting the sky or blowing up a super-volcano, spend Aura to treat a 6- as a 7 and a 7+ as a 10. Lose access to this move until you next roll a 10+ when you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Pray\|Pray]].

### Advanced Move: Channel Divinity
When you [[Moves/Combat Moves/All Out Attack\|All Out Attack]] an enemy that you have [[Moves/Basic Moves/Unleash Your Powers\|unleashed your powers on]], also roll with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ichor\|Ichor]] and take the roll of your choice.
