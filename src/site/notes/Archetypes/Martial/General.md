*You thrive in the heat of battle.*

### Starting Move: Feel the Flow
When you [[Moves/Combat Moves/Enter Battle\|Enter Battle]], use a Focus to dictate a sequence of three [[System Mechanics/Move\|basic and/or combat moves]] (e.g. Assess -> Secure an Edge -> All Out Attack). The first time those moves are made by any PC this combat, add +1 to their roll. At the end of the battle, all involved allies gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]] if they were able to successfully execute the sequence. If the sequence is interrupted, such as through an ally having to [[Moves/Basic Moves/Resist a Powerful Effect\|Resist a Powerful Effect]] or [[Moves/Core Moves/Face Death\|Face Death]], it resets.

### Advanced Move: Mirror
When you see someone use an [[Moves/Combat Moves/Secure an Edge\|edge]], mark a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] to roll + [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ether\|Ether]]. On a 7+, you may take advantage of the same opportunity even if it's temporary or complicated. On a 6-, describe how you falter. Gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].

### Advanced Move: Stay Calm
The first time you make a [[System Mechanics/Move\|move]] that requires a roll on a turn other than yours in a fight, choose one:
- Gain [[System Mechanics/Rolling Dice, Advantage, and Disadvantage\|advantage]] on the roll. 
- Gain a Focus.
- Gain an action.

### Advanced Move: Clear Mind
When you take decisive action to clear a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]], unlock and clear an additional condition at the end of the [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]].
