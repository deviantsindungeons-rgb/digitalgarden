*Nothing gets past you.*

### Starting Move: Keen Eye
When you [[Moves/Combat Moves/Secure an Edge\|Secure an Edge]] or [[Moves/Basic Moves/Assess\|Assess]], gain an Insight on a 10+. Use Insight to gain +1 to a roll with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ether\|Ether]] (once per roll). Insight disappears at the end of a [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]].

### Advanced Move: Interrogate
When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Assess\|Assess]] someone, you can also choose to ask if they've lied to you about anything recently. If they have, roll with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Humor\|Humor]]. On a 7+, you find an angle to press them further and learn what you want - gain a Focus and an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]]. On a 6-, learn where your investigation fell short - gain an Insight.

### Advanced Move: Channel Intelligence
When you [[Moves/Combat Moves/All Out Attack\|All Out Attack]] an enemy that you've [[Moves/Basic Moves/Assess\|assessed]], use a Focus or an Insight to roll with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ether\|Ether]] as well and take the roll of your choice.

### Advanced Move: Sound Mind
Use an Insight to reduce damage taken by your [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ether\|Ether]] (min. 1) when you [[System Mechanics/Combat\|exchange damage]].
