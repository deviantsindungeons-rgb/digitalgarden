*To call you neurotic would be an insult.*

### Starting Move: Tunnel Vision
While you have the maximum amount of Focuses you can have, ignore the penalties from being [[System Mechanics/Condition\|angry or drained]].

### Advanced Move: Talk Trash
When you tell an enemy how you're going to take them down to their face, mark a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] and roll with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Humor\|Humor]] when you [[Moves/Combat Moves/Enter Battle\|Enter Battle]]. Regardless of the result, you lose access to this move for the remainder of the [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]].
- On a 10+, choose two. 
	- The enemy takes 1 extra [[System Mechanics/Combat\|damage]] when you exchange damage with them for the duration of the fight.
	- Choose an additional good outcome when you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Unleash Your Powers\|Unleash Your Powers]] this battle.
	- When you or an ally [[Moves/Combat Moves/Secure an Edge\|Secures an Edge]] or [[Moves/Combat Moves/Take Control\|Takes Control of something]] from the enemy, gain a Focus.
- On a 7-9, choose one. 
- On a 6-, your bravado falters. Gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]] and resolve a [[Moves/Basic Moves/Unleash Your Powers\|bad outcome]]. 

### Advanced Move: Flash of Acuity
When you roll 10+ with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ether\|Ether]], choose an ally in the scene to impress. They gain one [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with you.

### Advanced Move: Cluttered Mind
While you're [[System Mechanics/Condition\|frightened]], gain +1 to rolls with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ether\|Ether]]. While you're [[System Mechanics/Condition\|guilty]], gain +1 to rolls with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Humor\|Humor]].
