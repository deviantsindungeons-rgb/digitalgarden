*When your day comes, you'll be too enraged to notice.*

### Starting Move: Call an Iso
When you [[Moves/Combat Moves/All Out Attack\|All Out Attack]] without anybody having [[Moves/Basic Moves/Provide Aid\|Provided Aid]] to you, gain [[System Mechanics/Rolling Dice, Advantage, and Disadvantage\|advantage]]. On a 12+, make an extra choice. You can make the same choice multiple times.

### Advanced Move: Unyielding Might
When you roll a [[System Mechanics/Move\|combat move]] while having thews remaining, mark a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] to treat a 6- as a 7.

### Advanced Move: Pump Up
When you perform some kind of pre-game ritual to psych yourself up, roll + [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Vigor\|Vigor]]. On a 7+, clear a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] and gain an extra thew at the start of the battle. On a 6-, describe why you feel ill-prepared. Gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].

### Advanced Move: Belly Drum
When you land the final blow on an enemy, unlock and clear a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]].
