*A warrior is only as good as what he is able to protect.*

### Starting Move: Intercept
In battle, use an action to lose [[System Mechanics/Health\|health]] in place of a PC that is very close to you or to [[Moves/Basic Moves/Resist a Powerful Effect\|Resist a Powerful Effect]] in their place. 

### Advanced Move: Draw Attention
When you [[System Mechanics/Combat\|exchange damage]] with a creature, mark a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] to choose an option as if you rolled a 7 to [[Moves/Combat Moves/Distract an Enemy\|Distract an Enemy]].

### Advanced Move: Second Wind
The first time you become [[System Mechanics/Combat\|bloodied]] in a [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]], heal a third of your maximum [[System Mechanics/Health\|health]] (rounded up) and clear a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]]. 

### Advanced Move: Steel Yourself
When you gather your inner resolve and prepare for a monumental task ahead, roll + [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ichor\|Ichor]]. On a 7+, find your nerve and unlock and clear a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]]. If you survive the encounter, gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]]. On a 6-, your doubts balloon - mark a condition. If you survive the encounter, gain two XP.
