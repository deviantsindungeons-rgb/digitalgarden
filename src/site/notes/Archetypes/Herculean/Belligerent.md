*Might makes right.*

### Starting Move: Fill the Data Bank
After [[System Mechanics/Combat\|exchanging damage]] with something, you can use [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Vigor\|Vigor]] in place of [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ether\|Ether]] when you attempt to [[Moves/Basic Moves/Assess\|Assess]] them or [[Moves/Combat Moves/Secure an Edge\|Secure an Edge]] against them in the future.

### Advanced Move: Ignore Etiquette
When you ignore etiquette in a discussion and draw attention to yourself, roll with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Humor\|Humor]]. 
- On a 7+, name someone in the [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]] who secretly agrees and is willing to help - gain +1 when you next try to [[Moves/Basic Moves/Exert Influence\|Exert Influence]] on them. 
- On a 6-, you make a fool of yourself. The GM describes how the perception of you changes. Gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].

### Advanced Move: Flash of Muscle
When you roll 10+ with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Vigor\|Vigor]], choose an ally in the scene to impress. They gain one [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with you.

### Advanced Move: Irrefutable Will
When you roll a 12+ to [[Moves/Combat Moves/Take Control\|Take Control]] of something by overwhelming them, make an extra choice. You can make the same choice multiple times.
