# Starting Move: Perform
You have a particular talent that you have carefully cultivated or trained. Describe what it is and how you got so good at it. Your talent, and your practice of it, brings Inspiration. The maximum amount of Inspiration you can have at a time is equal to your [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Humor\|Humor]] + 1 (min. 1). If you would gain Inspiration while at your maximum, spend it immediately. When you have a chance to perform, roll with an [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Attributes\|attribute]] that aligns with your talent. 
- On a 10+, gain three Inspiration. 
- On a 7-9, gain two Inspiration. 
- On a 6-, gain one Inspiration - the display might look good, but you're not satisfied. Mark a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]].

You can use Inspiration for any of the following (a PC can only benefit from one Inspiration per round):
- Reinforce - unlock a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] that a PC has.
- Encourage - grant a PC an extra action [[System Mechanics/Combat\|in a fight]].
- Soothe - restore two [[System Mechanics/Health\|health]] to a PC.
- Catalyze - gain a [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with another PC.
- (If you have the Pioneer Path) Invigorate - give a PC +2 to their roll when they make an [[System Mechanics/Move\|archetype move]].
- (If you have the Paragon Path) Bolster - give a PC +2 to their roll when they make a [[System Mechanics/Move\|basic move]].
- (If you have the Prophesied Path) Amp - give a PC +2 to their roll when they make a [[System Mechanics/Move\|combat move]].

# Divine Moves
*Start with all divine moves locked - you don't have access to them. After level 5, when you gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|advancement]], you can unlock a divine move. When you use it, lock it again.*
- Divine Determination. When you take damage that would kill you or force you to [[Moves/Core Moves/Face Death\|Face Death]], you may refuse it. Describe how your divine blood surges to keep you standing. Unlock and clear a condition and restore your [[System Mechanics/Health\|health]] until you are no longer [[System Mechanics/Combat\|bloodied]].
- Put on a Show. Describe how you passionately encourage a PC to make a difference. You both gain one [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with each other and they immediately unlock a divine move in one of their archetypes (other than this one).
- Enter Flow State. Describe how your talent enables you and your allies. For the rest of the [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]], you also gain the benefits from Inspiration when you use it on another PC.
