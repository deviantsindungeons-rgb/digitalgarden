*Folks can't help it but see things your way.*

### Starting Move: Change Views
When you roll 10+ to [[Moves/Basic Moves/Exert Influence\|Exert Influence]] on an NPC, you learn if they are truly open to change. If so, you can attempt to change their views. Roll, now or later. Regardless of the result, you cannot attempt to change their views again.
- On a 10+, they begin the journey to become their best self in earnest. 
- On a 7-9, they choose one instead.
	- Your words haunt and distract them, granting you and each PC in the [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]] [[System Mechanics/Rolling Dice, Advantage, and Disadvantage\|advantage]] on your next roll against them.
	- Your words connect with some part of them, and just this once they offer mercy, a secret, or an opportunity.
	- They reveal a burden of some kind that prevents them from changing. If you resolve it, then treat this result as a 10+.
- On a 6-, describe how your attempt falls flat and gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].

### Advanced Move: Touch Soul
When you successfully perform for someone, the display sits with them. You have [[System Mechanics/Rolling Dice, Advantage, and Disadvantage\|advantage]] when you next roll with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Humor\|Humor]] against them. 

### Advanced Move: See Things My Way
When a [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|bond-mate]] attempts to [[Moves/Basic Moves/Exert Influence\|Exert Influence]] on you, you can choose for them to roll with your depth with them instead of their depth with you. If you refuse on a 7+, don't mark a condition. If you do it anyway, gain an Inspiration.

### Advanced Move: Draw a Connection
When you successfully [[Moves/Combat Moves/Distract an Enemy\|Distract an Enemy]] with your talent, choose an option as if you rolled a 7 to [[Moves/Combat Moves/Secure an Edge\|Secure an Edge]].
