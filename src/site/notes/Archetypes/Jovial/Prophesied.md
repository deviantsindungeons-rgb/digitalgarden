*There's a touch of destiny about you.*

### Starting Move: Manifest Destiny
Once per [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]] when you perform, you may describe how you interweave your divine heritage into the performance and mark a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] to [[Moves/Basic Moves/Pray\|Pray]].

### Advanced Move: Isolate Yourself
When you would mark a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]], instead choose a [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|bond-mate]] in the scene and erase all current depth that you have with them (min. 1). Lose access to this move until you next roll a 10+ when you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Pray\|Pray]].

### Advanced Move: Gasp
Gain 3 Inspiration, ignoring your max for the rest of the scene. If you're in a fight, gain 1 Inspiration at the start of your next turn. Lose access to this move until you next roll a 10+ when you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Pray\|Pray]]. 

### Advanced Move: See the Future
When you start a session, secretly predict and write down an event that you think will come to pass in the future. When you [[Moves/Core Moves/End a Session\|End a Session]], also ask if your prediction came true. Lose access to this move until you next roll a 10+ when you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Pray\|Pray]].
