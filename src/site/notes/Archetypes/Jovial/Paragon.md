*You exemplify an ideal - something everyone tries, and fails, to live up to.*

### Starting Move: Show the Way
You have experience and wisdom beyond your years in a particular avenue. Choose an [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Attributes\|attribute]]. When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Provide Aid\|Provide Aid]] on an ally's [[System Mechanics/Move\|move]] that uses that attribute, you can also choose to treat a 6- as a 7.

### Advanced Move: Flash of Heart
When you roll 10+ with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Humor\|Humor]], choose an ally in the [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]] to impress. They gain one [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with you.

### Advanced Move: Daring Example
When you're about to try something incredibly risky in a high-stakes situation, roll + [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Humor\|Humor]]. On a 7+, describe something helpful that you can use, gaining +2 to the roll. On a 6-, notice a complication that makes things harder than you thought - resolve a [[Moves/Basic Moves/Unleash Your Powers\|bad outcome]].

### Advanced Move: Inherit Will
When you [[Moves/Core Moves/Face Death\|Face Death]] and choose to go out with a bang, you can also choose to name a PC and tell them why you believe in them. If they accept your words, they unlock this path and gain its starting move.
