Aside from things that a regular 21st century person would have, you might have some abstract items that will help you on your adventures.

### Gear
A pack with some useful tools. A single unit of gear has three uses. Expend a use to withdraw a mundane item that has some use to you, like a flashlight. You can keep the item.

### Supplies
Stuff you need to keep going. A single unit of supplies has three uses. When you spend a few minutes making use of your supplies, expend up to three uses and split that number between restored [[System Mechanics/Health\|health]] and restored uses to [[Items/Armour\|defensive]] or [[Items/Weapons\|offensive equipment]].
