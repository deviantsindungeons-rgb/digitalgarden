Weapons come in all shapes and sizes! All of them have a fictionally appropriate range and any number of properties. At base, a weapon deals 0-2 [[System Mechanics/Combat\|damage]]. However, you may be able to deal more, depending on the weapon itself, what you're swinging at, and the circumstances of the fiction. If you don't have a weapon, you're fighting unarmed - at base, deal 0 damage. 
### Weapon Properties:
- **Heavy**: Deal 1 extra damage if your [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Vigor\|Vigor]] is 2 or higher.
- **Precise**: Deal 1 extra damage if your [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Silver\|Silver]] is 2 or higher.
- **Technical**: Deal 1 extra damage if your [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ether\|Ether]] is 2 or higher.
- **Practiced**: Deal 1 extra damage if your [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Humor\|Humor]] is 2 or higher.
- **Blessed**: Deal 1 extra damage if your [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ichor\|Ichor]] is 2 or higher.
- **Gouging**: Deal 1 extra damage to bloodied enemies.
- **Piercing**: Deal 1 extra damage to armoured enemies.
- **Flanking**: Deal 1 extra damage to an enemy if an ally is very close to them.
- **Divine**: Deal 1 extra damage to demigods and monsters. This weapon cannot harm mortals.
- **Chosen**: Deal 1 extra damage if this weapon is sacred to your [[System Mechanics/Divine Heritage\|Divine Heritage]].
- **Messy**: Deal 1 damage to anything within melee range (other than you) when you deal damage.
- **Guarding**: Deal 1 less damage to reduce incoming damage by 1.
- **Swift**: Deal 1 extra damage after getting a running start.
- **Weird**: When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Unleash Your Powers\|Unleash Your Powers]], you can choose "This weapon deals 1 more damage when you next exchange damage" as a good outcome.
