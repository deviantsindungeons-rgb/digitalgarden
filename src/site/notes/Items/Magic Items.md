All kinds of unorthodox and reality warping paraphernalia. The Mist distorts most magic items enough that a regular mortal won't look twice at them. A curse tablet might resemble a stack of mesmerizing flyers. An amulet could pass as a fancy necklace. With the right [[System Mechanics/Divine Heritage\|divine heritage]], you may be able to get more uses or more potent effects out of these items.

### Ambrosia and Nectar
The sustenance of the gods. A single unit of ambrosia or nectar has three portions. When a portion is eaten (ambrosia) or drank (nectar), it restores a third of your maximum [[System Mechanics/Health\|health]] (rounded up) and you may clear a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]]. Ingesting more than three portions in the span of a few minutes instantly kills you. Three portions of a unit of ambrosia and nectar can be used to stabilize someone with [[Moves/Core Moves/Face Death\|grievous wounds]], restoring a third of their maximum health (rounded up).

### Amulet
The premier magic warding tool. It has one use which can be used to automatically resist and shrug off a minor magical effect. When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Resist a Powerful Effect\|resist a powerful magical effect]], it adds +1 to your roll.

### Curse Tablet
A thin sheet of lead with some runes carved into it, they are often left behind by magical users and can have very potent effects. Its exact number of uses and effects vary. With some downtime, you can figure out its exact effects and how to modify them to your liking.

### Satyr Instrument
Usually woodwinds, these are instruments with potent natural magic power. It has three uses, allowing you to conjure minor magical effects with natural resources nearby. For example, you could turn tree roots into a gnarled patch of terrain that is difficult to cross, or form a straitjacket out of a set of grapevines. 

### Greek Fire
Swirling green liquid in a flimsy canister. A unit of greek fire has one use. Expend a use to lob a deadly grenade far away, dealing 3 damage to anything very close to the point of impact, as well as any creature that moves into the area of effect. The fire lasts for a few minutes. It burns underwater. Certain monsters and immortal figures are resistant or outright immune to it.

### Hephaestus Trap
Marvels of engineering that the great god threw away. Its exact number of uses and effects vary. When you spend time setting up a trap, it becomes a dangerous, impeding hazard for anyone that enters its area of effect.

### Enchanted Ammunition
Left behind by the great Artemis on her epic hunts. It can come in many shapes and sizes, each having one use. A piece of enchanted ammunition can be used when you [[Moves/Combat Moves/All Out Attack\|All Out Attack]] to deal 3 extra damage.
