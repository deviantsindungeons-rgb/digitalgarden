Defensive equipment can be used in battle to mitigate damage. Armour is obscured by the Mist, so mortals won't look twice at it. With more control over the Mist, you can tailor your outfits to your wildest desires.

### Armour
Armour has a number of uses that reduce damage 1:1. You can wear a single set of armour at a time. Light armour has 1 use, medium armour has 2 uses, and heavy armour has 3 uses. If your [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Vigor\|Vigor]] is less than an armour's maximum amount of uses, you have [[System Mechanics/Rolling Dice, Advantage, and Disadvantage\|disadvantage]] on rolls made with [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Silver\|Silver]] while wearing it. Donning and doffing armour takes a couple seconds (an action).

### Shields
Shields have 2 uses that reduce damage 1:1. You can wield up to two shields at a time (if you don't want to hold a weapon).

### Helmet
Helmets have 1 use that reduce damage 1:1. You can wear a single helmet at a time. Corinthian helmets are popular fashion statements.
