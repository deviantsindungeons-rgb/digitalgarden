When you examine a person, place, or thing and try to uncover its secrets, say what you're trying to glean and roll + [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ether\|Ether]].
- On a 7+, ask two questions from the following list. The GM answers them and any follow-up questions honestly. On a 10+, gain +1 to the next roll you make when acting on what you learn.
	- What happened to X recently?
	- What is the best way in/out/through/around X?
	- When did X happen or when did it become this way?
	- Where did X come from?
	- What was X being used for?
	- Who or what is involved with X?
	- Where might X be located?
	- How can I get/use X to do something?
	- What opportunities can I get from X?
- On a 6-, ask one question and describe how you misinterpret or mistake it. Gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].
