When you use your powers to significantly change the situation, say how you do it and roll + [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ichor\|Ichor]].
- On a 10+, you are successful. The GM details how the situation has changed in your favour. Choose two good outcomes and one bad outcome. 
- On a 7+, the effect is temporary or comes with unintended consequences. You may mark a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] to ignore them. Choose one good outcome and one bad outcome. 
- On a 6-, things go terribly wrong - the GM details how. Choose two bad outcomes and gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].
### Good Outcomes
- Your powers exceed their limits and amplify their effects, increasing one of the options below by 1.
- Invigorate 1 ally in the [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]] and restore an aspect of their powers.
- Gain 1 current and maximum [[System Mechanics/Health\|health]] for the rest of the scene as strength courses through you.
- Deal 1 [[System Mechanics/Combat\|damage]] to an enemy in the scene as your powers run rampant.
- The next move made by 1 ally in the scene has [[System Mechanics/Rolling Dice, Advantage, and Disadvantage\|advantage]] as your awesomeness inspires them.
### Bad Outcomes
- An aspect of your powers becomes unusable for the rest of the scene as fatigue sets in.
- Take 1 damage as your powers rebound.
- Despair, enforcing disadvantage on your next move.
- Betray an ally's expectations, losing 1 [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with them.
- Your powers terrify you, locking a marked condition.
