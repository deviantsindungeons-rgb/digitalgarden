When you find yourself in a place of power and make a meaningful sacrifice to seek guidance from a divine figure, say how you do it and roll + [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ichor\|Ichor]]. Depending on your relationship with the divine figure, your own [[System Mechanics/Divine Heritage\|divine heritage]], and the circumstances of your situation, the GM may impose advantage or disadvantage on your roll.
- On a 7+, you get the answers you need, but they may be cryptic or confusing. 
- On a 10+, the answers come with a useful gift. 
- On a 6-, what you learn is ambiguous at best and dubious at worst. Mark a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] and gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].
