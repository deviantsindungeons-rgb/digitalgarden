When you reach maximum [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with another PC, erase all your depth at the end of the [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]] and choose one of the options below. Afterwards, increase the maximum possible depth between you and this PC by one.
- Choose a new bond and both you and your bond-mate gain access to its starting move. If you shared an old bond, erase it and lose access to its moves. You and your bond-mate gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]] for each advanced move that it had.
- Reinforce an existing bond, choosing an advanced move from it. Both you and your bond-mate gain access to the move.
- Ask the player a question about their character's thoughts, feelings, or motivations. They must answer honestly and can choose to do the same to you. You both gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].
