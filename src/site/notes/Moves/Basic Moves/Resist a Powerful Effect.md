When you are subjected to a particularly powerful attack or suffer significant damage, roll + [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Vigor\|Vigor]] or [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ichor\|Ichor]]. You suffer a penalty equal to the number of your marked [[System Mechanics/Condition\|conditions]].
- On a 10+, stand tall as you explain how you weather the blow. Unlock and clear a condition. Gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].
- On a 7-9, choose one:
	- You lash out, provoking another player character to take foolhardy action or mark a condition. You both lose a [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with each other.
	- You give ground, giving your enemy an opportunity.
	- You struggle through the pain, marking two conditions.
- On a 6-, choose one:
	- You must remove yourself from the situation - passing out, fleeing, etc.
	- You lose control of yourself and your powers in a disastrous way - describe how.
	- Lose [[System Mechanics/Health\|health]] until you [[Moves/Core Moves/Face Death\|Face Death]].
