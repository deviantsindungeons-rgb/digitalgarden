When you spend time talking with someone, whether it's in the heat of battle or during a moment of reprieve, and attempt to help them work through their issues, answer the following yes-no questions and roll with +1 for each yes:
- Did they open up about something they're going through?
- Did they reveal how a condition they currently have is affecting them?
- Are you currently safe?

No matter the result, they feel better and clear a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] of their choice. 
- On a 10+, they clear an additional condition or gain a [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with you, their choice. 
- On a 6-, you mark a condition or lose a depth with them, your choice.
