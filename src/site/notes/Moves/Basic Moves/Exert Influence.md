When you can feasibly use your understanding or relationship with someone to influence them into doing something, say how you do it and roll + [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Humor\|Humor]].
- On a 10+, they do it without question. 
- On a 7+, they do it with some stipulations.
- On a 6-, they shut you down and inflict a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]]. Gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].

If you exert influence over another PC, roll + their [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with you instead of Humor.
- On a 7+, they are compelled to do as you say. If they refuse, they mark a condition and erase a depth with you.
- On a 6-, they shut you down and inflict a condition. Erase a depth with them.
