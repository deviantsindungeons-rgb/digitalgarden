When you act despite an imminent threat or obstacle, describe what you do and roll +...
- [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Vigor\|Vigor]] if you break through, overpower, or physically endure it.
- [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Silver\|Silver]] if you avoid, dodge, or outpace it.
- [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ether\|Ether]] if you analyze, scrutinize, or predict it.
- [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Humor\|Humor]] if you trick, misdirect, or manipulate it.

On a 10+, you get it done, but choose one. On a 7-9, you get it done but choose two:
- It impacts you. Mark a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] or lose a [[System Mechanics/Health\|health]].
- Doing so escalates the situation or draws unwanted attention. The GM explains how after you choose this option.
- You have to sacrifice something to do it - name what it is. The GM might demand more.

On a 6-, describe how it goes wrong. Gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].
