When you attempt to seize power over something from an enemy (item, position, authority, etc.), say what it is and how you plan to do it. If your objective is protected or your approach is infeasible, the GM may require you to first [[Moves/Basic Moves/Defy Danger\|Defy Danger]] or [[Moves/Combat Moves/Secure an Edge\|Secure an Edge]].
- If you overwhelm them with force, roll + [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Vigor\|Vigor]], but they may respond and attack you.
- If you outmaneuver them, roll + [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Silver\|Silver]], but they might take something in turn.
- If you outsmart or trick them, roll + [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ether\|Ether]] or [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Humor\|Humor]], but they might hold a grudge.

On a 7+, you take it. Choose one of the following below and they retaliate based on your approach. On a 10+, make an extra choice or avoid their retaliation.
- They can't try to take it back anytime soon.
- You revel in victory, clearing a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]].
- If you rolled with Vigor, [[System Mechanics/Combat\|exchange damage]].

On a 6-, you fail and they retaliate anyway. Gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].
