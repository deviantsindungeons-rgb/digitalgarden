When you leverage an edge or vulnerability to directly engage and attack an enemy, roll + [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Vigor\|Vigor]] and [[System Mechanics/Combat\|exchange damage]]. On a 7+, choose one below. On a 10+, choose two.
- Make it hurt and deal 1 extra damage.
- Mitigate their blow, taking 1 less damage.
- Let off some steam and clear a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]].

On a 6-, they deal 2 extra damage to you. Gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].
