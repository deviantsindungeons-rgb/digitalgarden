 When a charged situation turns into a fight, prepare for the worst and roll + [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Silver\|Silver]] or [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ether\|Ether]]. The result determines your place in the turn order and how many actions you can take on your first turn. On a tie, you and the other PC can decide who goes first.
- On a 10+, you have 3 actions on your first turn.
- On a 7+, you have 2 actions on your first turn.
- On a 6-, you have 1 action on your first turn. Gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].
