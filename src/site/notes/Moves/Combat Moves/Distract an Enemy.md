When you preoccupy an enemy or hold them off, say how you do it and roll +...
- [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Vigor\|Vigor]] if you engage them directly, but they might attack you.
- [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Silver\|Silver]] if you harass them from afar, but they might trap or trick you.
- [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Humor\|Humor]] if you provoke them with cutting words, but they might escalate the situation.

On a 7+, you do it. Choose one of the following below and they retaliate based on your approach. On a 10+, make an extra choice or avoid their retaliation.
- Impede them. Negate an advantage, opportunity, resistance, or something they were about to do.
- Occupy them. Choose an ally to gain an action.
- Weaken them. They deal 1 less damage when they next deal damage.

On a 6-, they overpower or outmaneuver you. Gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].
