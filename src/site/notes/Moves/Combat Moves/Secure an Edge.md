When you survey the battlefield or seek an advantage, whether it be a weak spot in the enemy's defences or a useful tactical tool, say how you do it and roll +...
- [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Ether\|Ether]] if you recall something or scan the environment.
- [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Humor\|Humor]] if you study your enemy or probe their emotions.
- If you knew something in advance, say what it is and forgo the roll, treating the result as a 7.

On a 7+, choose one below. On a 7-9, after you choose, the GM will describe how the edge is temporary or complicated.
- You find a flaw in the enemy's protections, behaviours, or techniques, which you or an ally can exploit to [[Moves/Combat Moves/All Out Attack\|All Out Attack]].
- You find something useful around you, and can use it to [[Moves/Basic Moves/Provide Aid\|Provide Aid]] without marking a condition.
- Find a moment of reprieve and spend an action to clear a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]].

On a 6-, your efforts are fruitless and the situation escalates. Gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].
