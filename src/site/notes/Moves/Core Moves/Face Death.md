When you reach zero [[System Mechanics/Health\|Health]], choose one:
- Go out with a bang. Take control of the [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]] and describe how you significantly improve the situation. Afterwards, die.
- Break. Choose a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] that you currently have and lock it. If you don't have any, mark a condition and lock it. Suffer grievous wounds - fall unconscious and remain at zero health. If you don't receive medical attention by the end of the scene, die.
- Gamble with death. Roll. Other PCs can [[Moves/Basic Moves/Provide Aid\|Provide Aid]], but it requires significant effort and their full attention.
	- On a 10+, you'll live - for now. Regain one health. You can act normally, but you might have some injures.
	- On a 7-9, the GM says what part of you doesn't survive. Accept it and live, or hold on to it and die. Regain one health. You can act normally, but you might have some injures. At the end of the scene, decrease an [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Attributes\|attribute]] of your choice by 1 (min. -3).
	- On a 6-, your time is up. What are your last words?
