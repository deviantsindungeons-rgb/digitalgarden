When you mark your last [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]], you reach your limit. Any moves that trigger resolve and you face your inner demons. Erase up to three [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] across any of your relationships and roll + that number, resolve its effects, then unlock and clear a marked condition at the end of the [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]]:
- On a 10+, rise to the occasion and unlock and clear a marked condition.
- On a 7-9, bottle your emotions and lock a marked condition.
- On a 6-, you must remove yourself from the situation - passing out, fleeing, etc. Decrease an [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Attributes\|attribute]] of your choice by 1 (min. -3).
