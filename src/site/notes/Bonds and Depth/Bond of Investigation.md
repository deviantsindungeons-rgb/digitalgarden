*Whether it's solving neighbourhood crimes or unraveling the mysteries of the cosmos, your minds work particularly well together.*

### Starting Move: Open a Case
When you start a session, you and your bond-mate may ask one shared question about a companion, mystery, aspect of the world, etc. When you find an answer during the same session, you both clear a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]]. If you overcame significant obstacles to find it, you also gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].

### Advanced Move: Digging Deeper
When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Assess\|Assess]] with your bond-mate’s [[Moves/Basic Moves/Provide Aid\|aid]], ask double the amount of questions.

### Advanced Move: Just Like We Planned
When you [[Moves/Combat Moves/Enter Battle\|Enter Battle]] with your bond-mate and you would have less actions on your first turn, set your action count to theirs.

### Advanced Move: Good Cop, Bad Cop
When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Exert Influence\|Exert Influence]] with your bond-mate’s [[Moves/Basic Moves/Provide Aid\|aid]] while interrogating someone, either of you can mark [[System Mechanics/Condition\|angry]] to treat a 6- as a 7.
