*You share a passion for a topic, hobby, craft, or similar, and spend most of your time together discussing or working on it. Decide together what interest you share.*

### Starting Move: Great Minds
When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Provide Aid\|Provide Aid]] to your bond-mate in relation to your interest, you can also choose to substitute their [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Attributes\|attribute]] with yours for the roll.

### Advanced Move: Paired Project
You’re working on a shared masterpiece or discovery related to your interest. Say what it is, and the GM will give you one to three requirements from the list below. When you meet the requirements, you finish the project and each gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]]. Lose access to this move until you unlock it again.
- It’s going to take days/weeks/months.
- You'll need to learn X first.
- You'll need access to X.
- You’ll need help from X.
- You’ll need to utilize X.

### Advanced Move: Study Buddies
When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Assess\|Assess]] something related to your interest, on a 12+ your bond-mate may answer a question instead of the GM.

### Advanced Move: Specialists
When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Defy Danger\|Defy Danger]] in a matter related to your interest, ignore the penalties of any conditions that affect either you or your bond-mate. On a 12+, choose 0.
