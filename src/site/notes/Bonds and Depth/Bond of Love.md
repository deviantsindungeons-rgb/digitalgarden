*You both have mutual feelings of romance, attraction, or similar affection, and have agreed to explore them together.*

### Starting Move: Get a Room
When you and your bond-mate spend time and intimacy together, you both reset to one heartstring each. You can spend a heartstring to [[Moves/Basic Moves/Provide Aid\|Provide Aid]] to your bond-mate without marking a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]], regardless of distance or other obstacles.

### Advanced Move: Words of Affirmation
When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Support\|Support]] your bond-mate, you may also ask a yes-no question about your relationship. They unlock and clear an extra [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] no matter the result.

### Advanced Move: Honeymoon
When you get a room, reset to two heartstrings instead of one.

### Advanced Move: I Do
When you and your bond-mate vow a lifelong commitment to each other, briefly take total shared control of the narrative, accomplish one thing together that dramatically changes the world or each other, then lose access to this move forever. You both gain two [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].
