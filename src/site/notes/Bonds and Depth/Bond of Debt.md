*One of you owes the other, whether it's money, a period of servitude, gas money, or something else entirely.*

### Starting Move: I See You
Designate one of you as the debtor and one of you as the loaner. Discuss the details of your contract. When this bond is forged, the debtor forgoes their next [[System Mechanics/XP\|advancement]]. Whenever an advanced move in this bond is unlocked, the loaner gains an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]]. When a new bond is forged and this bond is erased, the debtor gains three [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]] for each advanced move it had instead of one.

### Advanced Move: I Need More Time
While the debtor and loaner are in the same [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]], the debtor gains +1 to rolls made to [[Moves/Basic Moves/Exert Influence\|Exert Influence]] and the loaner gains +1 to rolls made to [[Moves/Basic Moves/Defy Danger\|Defy Danger]].

### Advanced Move: You Owe Me
When the debtor [[Moves/Basic Moves/Exert Influence\|Exerts Influence]] on the loaner by leveraging the debt, the loaner cannot refuse on a 7+.

### Advanced Move: Not On My Watch
When the loaner [[Moves/Core Moves/Reach Your Limit\|Reaches Their Limit]], the debtor can mark a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] to let them unlock and clear a condition no matter the result. On a 6-, the loaner does not have to remove themselves from the scene.
