*The two of you have a pet of some kind. The two of you have made an oath to protect it with your lives.*

### Starting Move: Best Friend
When you gain this bond, describe your pet - what it is, how you found it, and what it means to each of you. When your pet is with you, you usually control and describe their choices and actions. When they aren’t with you, the GM controls them. Your pet has two characteristics, one chosen by you and the other by your bond-mate, on top of any pre-existing characteristics it may have:
- **Bulky:** While your pet is with you, mark a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] to have it lose [[System Mechanics/Health\|health]] or [[Moves/Basic Moves/Resist a Powerful Effect\|Resist a Powerful Effect]] in your place.
- **Cute:** When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Support\|Support]] a PC, additionally ask if they think your pet is cute. On a 10+, they clear an additional condition _and_ gain a [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with you.
- **Haunted:** (This characteristic can only be chosen after your bond-mate dies). Choose an [[System Mechanics/Move\|archetype move]] that your bond-mate had. While your pet is with you, gain access to that move. Its intrinsic limitations and conditions still apply.
- **Holy:** While your pet is with you, gain +1 to rolls when you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Pray\|Pray]] or [[Moves/Basic Moves/Unleash Your Powers\|Unleash Your Powers]].
- **Loved:** While your pet is with you, gain [[System Mechanics/Rolling Dice, Advantage, and Disadvantage\|advantage]] when you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Exert Influence\|Exert Influence]].
- **Loyal:** Once per [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]], while your pet is with you, you can also choose the following when you [[Moves/Core Moves/Face Death\|Face Death]]: Have your pet rescue you as if you rolled a 10 on Gamble with Death.
- **Reliable:** While your pet is with you, suffer -2 to rolls from being [[System Mechanics/Condition\|frightened]] or [[System Mechanics/Condition\|angry]] instead of -3.
- **Slippery:** While your pet is with you, choose one less option when you successfully [[Moves/Basic Moves/Defy Danger\|Defy Danger]].
- **Vicious:** While your pet is with you, deal 1 extra [[System Mechanics/Combat\|damage]] when you exchange damage.
- **Wicked:** While your pet is with you, make an extra choice when you [[Moves/Combat Moves/Take Control\|Take Control]] of something material.

Your pet has [[System Mechanics/Health\|Health]] equal to the average of you and your bond-mate's levels (rounded up). It doesn't have any conditions and cannot gain any. It also cannot [[Moves/Core Moves/Reach Your Limit\|Reach Its Limit]]. This bond persists if your bond-mate dies. If your pet dies, immediately erase this bond and replace it with the [[Bonds and Depth/Bond of Trauma\|Bond of Trauma]].

### Advanced Move: Playtime
When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Provide Aid\|Provide Aid]] to your bond-mate and your pet is with you, do so without marking a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]].

### Advanced Move: Stay Away!
When your companion is threatened or harmed, you may immediately [[Moves/Combat Moves/Distract an Enemy\|Distract an Enemy]] without spending an action. On a 10+ make an extra choice *and* avoid their retaliation.

### Advanced Move: Evolve
Choose an additional characteristic for your pet to gain or work with the GM to give it a custom characteristic. Unlock this move.
