*One of you protects the other and teaches them your ways. Perhaps about life or philosophy; or perhaps about greek fire.*

### Starting Move: Support System
Designate one of you as the mentor and one as the ward. When the mentor [[Moves/Basic Moves/Support\|Supports]] the ward, on a 7+ the ward gains an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]]. When the ward's level increases, the mentor gains an XP.

### Advanced Move: Sincere Flattery
When the ward imitates a mannerism or ability from the mentor, either may mark a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] for the ward to gain access to one of the mentor’s moves for the rest of the [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]].

### Advanced Move: Just Like Practice
When either the ward or the mentor [[Moves/Basic Moves/Provide Aid\|Provide Aid]] to the other, on a 12+ both clear a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] or gain one [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with the other.

### Advanced Move: Heroic Sacrifice 
When the ward would [[Moves/Core Moves/Face Death\|Face Death]], the mentor may take their place, unlocking and clearing a condition and joining the scene if they were absent. When the mentor dies, the ward gains one of their [[System Mechanics/Move\|archetype moves]] and retains it after this bond is erased.
