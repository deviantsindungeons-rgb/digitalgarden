*The two of you have been through some real shit. Whether you like it or not, your bond-mate is the only one that understands.*

### Starting Move: Dwell
When you gain this bond, choose a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] and change it to bereaved. When you would mark that condition, mark bereaved instead. You don't suffer the previous condition's penalties while bereaved. Instead, while bereaved, take -1 when you roll a non-core [[System Mechanics/Move\|move]]. To clear it decisively, break down and let it all out. You may lose [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with other PCs as per the GM's discretion. 

When you [[Moves/Core Moves/Reach Your Limit\|Reach Your Limit]] or [[Moves/Core Moves/Face Death\|Face Death]], unlock and clear bereaved no matter the result. Furthermore, you don't suffer a penalty equal to your marked conditions when you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Resist a Powerful Effect\|Resist a Powerful Effect]]. 

### Advanced Move: Share a Moment
When you gain [[Moves/Basic Moves/Support\|Support]] from another PC and reflect on a memory related to your trauma, they gain a [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with you no matter the result.

### Advanced Move: Catharsis
While you're bereaved and you roll a 10+ on any move, you may describe how your inner turmoil elevates you to greater heights. Clear a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]]. If your bond-mate witnesses the event, they may also clear a condition.

### Advanced Move: Move On
When you [[Moves/Core Moves/End a Session\|End a Session]], additionally ask if you or your bond-mate are bereaved. If you have access to this move when you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Forge a Bond\|Forge a Bond]] and erase this bond, gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|advancement]].
