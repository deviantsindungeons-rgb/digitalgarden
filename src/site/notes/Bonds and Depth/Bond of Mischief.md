*You crave laughter and entertainment, especially when together. What’s a little troublemaking in its pursuit?*

### Starting Move: Pull a Prank
Once per session, after you accomplish something for the sole purpose of entertainment or chaos, choose one. If your bond-mate helped, they choose one as well.
- It’s captivating - choose a PC that witnessed it - they gain a [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with you.
- It’s impressive - gain +1 when you next try to [[Moves/Basic Moves/Exert Influence\|Exert Influence]] on someone that witnessed it.
- It’s distracting - gain an action if you're in battle.

### Advanced Move: Bamboozle
When you and your bond-mate are in battle, both of you may mark a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] to create a significant twist (as if you had [[Archetypes/Apollonian/Mechanist\|Manifest a Miracle]]). Lose access to this move until you unlock it again.

### Advanced Move: Hoodwink
When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Defy Danger\|Defy Danger]], you can always bring your bond-mate with you and cover them with the same roll. If they [[Moves/Basic Moves/Provide Aid\|Provide Aid]] to you, choose one less on a 7+.

### Advanced Move: Love to Make You Smile
When you pull a prank, you can both also choose:
- It’s inspiring - gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].
