*Constantly pushing each other to greater heights, or longer falls, each of you refuses to be outdone by the other.*

### Starting Move: Always a Competition
When you pull off an impressive feat or dangerous trick, ask your rival if they are moved. If so, you start winning. If not, you may erase a [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with them to treat it like they do. Who needs their opinion anyways? When you start a session, no one is winning. When you [[Moves/Core Moves/End a Session\|End a Session]], additionally ask if you're winning.

### Advanced Move: Lean Forward
When you start winning, you may taunt your rival. If you do, clear a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] and they gain [[System Mechanics/Rolling Dice, Advantage, and Disadvantage\|advantage]] on their next roll.

### Advanced Move: Check the Score
Once per session, when you admit that your rival went above and beyond or did the impossible, gain a [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with them; they either gain a depth with you or unlock and clear a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] (their choice).

### Advanced Move: Show 'Em How It's Done
When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Provide Aid\|Provide Aid]] to your rival by acting like them, you can also choose: 
- When they roll, you also roll. If your result is higher, you start winning and they use your roll instead.
