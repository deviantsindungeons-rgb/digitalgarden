*Sibling, parent, cousin, friend. Through circumstance or blood, you know, love, and bicker with each other like no one else.*

### Starting Move: Birds of a Feather
Where one of you goes, so does the other. When you gain [[System Mechanics/Rolling Dice, Advantage, and Disadvantage\|advantage]] or +1 on a roll as a result of another move, your bond-mate gets the same benefits.

### Advanced Move: Remember When...
When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Provide Aid\|Provide Aid]] to your bond-mate by referencing your shared history, choose an additional benefit as long as you don’t elaborate on the reference.

### Advanced Move: Come Running
When your bond-mate is in trouble, you may appear next to them here and now, no questions asked (yet). If you were trapped, endangered, or impeded, the GM will first say a cost between one and ten, depending on how bad a position you were in. Split that number as you choose between lost [[System Mechanics/Health\|health]] and marked [[System Mechanics/Condition\|conditions]], then get to them anyways.

### Advanced Move: Lean on Me
When you would mark a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] in the same [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]] as your bond-mate, they may choose to mark one instead.
