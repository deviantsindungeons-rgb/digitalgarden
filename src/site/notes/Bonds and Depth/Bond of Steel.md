*You’ve fought against foes neither of you could defeat alone, but together you triumphed more times than you can count.*

### Starting Move: Adrenaline Junkies
When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Provide Aid\|Provide Aid]] to your bond-mate on a [[System Mechanics/Move\|combat move]], you may lose a [[System Mechanics/Health\|health]] instead of marking a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]]. You may also provide aid multiple times at once, paying the cost each time.

### Advanced Move: Training Exercise
When you prepare for a battle ahead, you and your bond-mate can choose one [[System Mechanics/Move\|combat move]] together (other than enter battle). During your next battle, add “Gain an [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]]” to the list of choices available for that move. Additionally, the first time each battle that one of you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Provide Aid\|Provides Aid]] to the other in that move, it doesn’t cost a condition.

### Advanced Move: Slug It Out
When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Support\|Support]] your bond-mate by duelling, brawling, or otherwise fighting with them, you may ignore the questions and roll + [[System Mechanics/Attributes/Vigor\|Vigor]]. On a 10+, clear a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]].

### Advanced Move: Take the Shot
When you [[Moves/Combat Moves/All Out Attack\|All Out Attack]] with your bond-mate’s [[Moves/Basic Moves/Provide Aid\|aid]], or with an [[Moves/Combat Moves/Secure an Edge\|edge they secured]], on a 10+ you may also choose to avoid all damage.
