*When it comes to values, morals, beliefs, and perhaps faith, the two of you are of similar minds.*

### Starting Move: Shared Alignment
When you gain this bond, discuss and write down a statement you both fervently agree on (e.g. we will protect the innocent, this oath is unbreakable, this monster must be slain). This is your alignment. When you [[Moves/Core Moves/End a Session\|End a Session]], additionally ask if you reinforced your alignment or advanced its goals. If the answer is no, you may mark a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]] to rewrite the alignment.

### Advanced Move: Zealotry
When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Provide Aid\|Provide Aid]] to your bond-mate in pursuit of your alignment, do so without marking a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]].

### Advanced Move: Believe in Me
When you reach [[Moves/Core Moves/Face Death\|Face Death]] while fighting for your alignment, you may choose to go out with a bang and get its benefit without dying. Then you lose access to this move forever.

### Advanced Move: Return the Favour
When you help or save an NPC in a pivotal moment (e.g. saving their life, helping them with a [[System Mechanics/Quest\|Quest]], buying them a house), you can [[Moves/Basic Moves/Exert Influence\|Exert Influence]] on them. On a 12+, they support your alignment. Once per NPC, you may call upon them; they will appear and help with the current situation.
