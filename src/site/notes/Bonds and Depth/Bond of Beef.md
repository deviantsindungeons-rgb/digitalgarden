*It’s a wonder the two of you work together at all, considering how little you can stand of each other.*

### Starting Move: Sabotage
When you indirectly (or directly) work against your bond-mate, you may enforce [[System Mechanics/Rolling Dice, Advantage, and Disadvantage\|disadvantage]] on a roll they make. If you do, clear a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]]. Advanced moves from this bond are individual, not shared. When your bond-mate gains a move from this bond, gain two [[System Mechanics/XP\|XP]].

### Advanced Move: Let 'Em Know
After you argue with your bond-mate, answer the following yes-no questions, then roll with +1 for each yes:
- Did you put most of the fault or blame on them?
- Do you feel that you won the argument?
- Are you currently safe?

On any result you clear a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]]. On a 10+, you clear another condition or gain one [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with them, your choice. On a 6- they mark a condition or lose one depth with you, their choice.

### Advanced Move: Maybe It's Me
When you [[Moves/Basic Moves/Assess\|Assess]] someone, you can also choose to ask who they like more between you and your bond-mate. If it's you, unlock and clear a [[System Mechanics/Condition\|condition]].

### Advanced Move: Dream Team
When you put your mutual distain aside and work together toward a common goal, until the end of the [[System Mechanics/Scene\|scene]] you can spend a [[Bonds and Depth/Relationships, Depth, and Bonds\|depth]] with your bond-mate to upgrade a roll from 6- to 7 or from 7+ to 12.
